Context

What the demo is showing

Motivation

CREDO asks whether a prescribed decision remains near-optimal after the uncertain outcome is realized, rather than only asking which decision a prediction pipeline returns.

Framework

For a candidate z, the demo shades outcomes where z is near-optimal for an editable feasible region Z, then estimates the mass outside that region.

Results

The paper studies conformal decision-risk assessment more broadly. This page keeps the interactive piece limited to the simplified 2D linear case.
